    Our Project features

    .Sales performance:-Visual representation of total Sales revenue, profit margins,trends over time.

    .Product Analysis:-Top-Selling products based on revenue, quality,quantity sold and product performance trends and comparisons to its historical data.

    .Departmental Analysis:-Contribution of each departments to overall Sales revenue.Identify high performing departments.

    .Store performance:-comparisons of Sales performance across different cities.and Analyse the best store performance cities and areas.

    .Trend-Analysis:-Identify the trends of sales, peaks and troughs.and keep anlysing it.

    .Improved customer Experience:-Enhance the customer experience by offering personalized promotions, products-recommendation, and targeted marketing initiatives.

    .Sales and Revenue:- Total Sales: Daily, weekly, monthly, yearly figures. Revenue Breakdown: By product category and region. Top-Selling Products: Best-selling items and their revenue. Inventory Management Customer Demographics: Age, gender, day, time, month of day.
    Customer Feedback: Reviews, ratings, and satisfaction scores. Store Performance: Foot traffic and in-store sales. Order Fulfillment: Processing times and return rates. Marketing and Promotions
    Profit Margins: Gross and net profit margins.
